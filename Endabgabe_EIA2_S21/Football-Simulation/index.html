Instructions

In this form, you can determine the properties of your teams such as velocity and precision ranges. In addition, you are free to choose each teams color.

In the simulation, as soon as a player is in possession of the ball, you can decide where they will aim to kick it. Depending on the players individual skills, wich will be randomly decided within the range of your chosen values, the ball will hit its target or not. The distance also plays a role.

By clicking on a player, you can see their info.
You can also switch players for new ones.

The higher the value of precision, the more likely the target will be missed. The higher the value of velocity, the faster the player will be.

Gameplay

Click on a player to see their info. Double click on the field to shoot the ball.
